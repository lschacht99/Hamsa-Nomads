The retreat experience

A warm Shavuos retreat with meaningful programming, relaxed social time, meals together, and space to enjoy the Vermont setting.

Thursday night learning

Arrive, settle in, meet the group, and ease into the retreat with a relaxed learning night and warm opening atmosphere.

Shabbos & Yom Tov meals

Shared kosher meals, meaningful conversation, singing, divrei Torah, and a calm Shabbos and Yom Tov atmosphere.

Games & connection

Board games, casual hangouts, discussion groups, and unstructured time to connect naturally with other guests.

The place

A private Vermont home surrounded by nature.

Beautiful shared spaces, mountain views, cozy gathering areas, and room to breathe — the setting is part of what makes this Shavuos feel slower, softer, and more memorable.

Weekend rhythm

The weekend is designed to feel full but not overwhelming — a balance of Yom Tov, rest, connection, and the beauty of Vermont.

Shavuos night

Arrival, settling in, opening learning night, coffee and tea, relaxed introductions, and preparing for Shabbos together.

Shabbos

Meals, davening, discussions, rest, games, and time to enjoy the group in a slower, meaningful setting.

Sunday hike

A Vermont nature outing or hike, plus morning time for the hot tub, game room, conversations, and relaxing on the property.

Included atmosphere

Kosher food, minyan, eiruv, Shabbos and Yom Tov environment, private home, mountain views, and curated group energy.
