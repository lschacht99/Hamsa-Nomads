About the retreat

A Shavuos that feels curated, not overproduced.

This retreat is for people who want something more personal than a standard program: a beautiful private house, a smaller group, and a weekend that feels both intentional and easy.

The structure is there, but the energy stays soft. There is time for meals, davening, learning, games, conversation, fresh air, and the slower rhythm that makes Yom Tov feel like Yom Tov.
